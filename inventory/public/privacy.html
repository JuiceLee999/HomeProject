Juice Lee Productions

This Privacy Policy applies to the SHIT web application and all related services. It is compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and applicable US privacy law.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address — used for login and account identification
  • Password — stored exclusively as a bcrypt hash; we never store or transmit your plaintext password

2.2 Inventory Data (content you create)

  • Item names, descriptions, categories, locations, quantities, values, brands, models, serial numbers, condition, notes, and tags
  • Item photos (stored as base64-encoded image data on our servers)
  • Attached documents (receipts, manuals, warranties)
  • Checkout records: borrower names, destinations, dates
  • Lists and list memberships
  • Purchase dates

2.3 Technical Data

  • JWT session tokens — short-lived tokens stored in your browser's localStorage for authentication; expire after 7 days
  • Server logs — standard web server logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics services. We do not collect behavioral or usage analytics.

4. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account
  • To store and display your inventory data
  • To generate QR codes linked to your items
  • To enable sharing features (lists shared with other users)
  • To investigate security incidents and prevent abuse

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. AI-POWERED FEATURES

The Service includes optional AI features (auto-generated item descriptions, image analysis) powered by third-party AI providers:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini) — policies.google.com/privacy

When you use an AI feature, the relevant item text and/or image is transmitted to the selected provider's API. This data is processed under their respective privacy policies. We do not send your email address or account credentials to AI providers. AI features are optional and can be skipped.

Legal basis for this transfer: your explicit consent at the time of using the feature (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

6. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Hosting infrastructure: Your data is stored on servers provided by our hosting provider. They act as a data processor under a data processing agreement and do not have independent access to your data.
  • AI providers: As described in Section 5, only when you explicitly use AI features.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Juice Lee Productions or others.

7. DATA RETENTION

  • Account and inventory data: Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted account data: Purged within 30 days of account deletion
  • Server logs: Retained up to 30 days, then automatically deleted

8. DATA SECURITY

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard, one-way)
  • Authentication uses signed JWT tokens with a 7-day expiry
  • Logout invalidates all active sessions
  • Rate limiting on authentication and API endpoints to prevent brute force attacks
  • HTTP security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options) via Helmet

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
